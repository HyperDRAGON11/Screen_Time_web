Download Screen Time app

  1. Click the button below to go to the app download page.
  2. On the website, click on the blue bar, which says 'Screen Time'.
  3. Open the APK, a pop-up will appear. Click on Install.
  4. The app will appear on your home screen. If the app does not appear, search the app.
  5. Open the app
  6. Click on the 'Authorize' button on the first screen. grant permissions.
  7. Now you are ready to use the app!
